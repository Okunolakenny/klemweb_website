About Us

Klemweb is a limited liability company that specializes in training youngsters to deploy in demand skills and offering technlogical solution to companies, organizations and businesses.

We certainly believe that technology is one of the keys to Africa's development and as such we are investing time and resources to enable Africans to grow Technologically.

Klemweb became a registered ICT Institute in the year 2020. We have spotted opportunities in Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Digital Marketting, Data Analysis, Product Design. We also train people that are passionate about becoming tech expert to actualize their goals by impacting them how to sell their services.

Our Vision

  1. To train over One Milion Youngstars to deploy in-demand skills to bridge the digital gap to meet the growing technological clamour in Africa

Our mission

  1. To plant Branches all over Africa.
  2. To make learning easy at affordable Rates.
  3. To employ first-class tools to raise youngsters that will be at the vanguard of technology across africa and with high flying representation in the world.
  4. To reposition youngsters mindset towards ethical modalities of modern technology.
