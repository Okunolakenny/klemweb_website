WHO WE ARE

Klemweb Institute was founded in 2019 by Adetoye Abiodun with the visionof raising over one million youngsters to deploy in-demand skills to brigde the digital gap to meet the growing technological clamour in Africa.

Klemweb became a registered organization specialized in IT Training in the year 2020. We have spotted opportunities in branding,software development,web development,networking,cyber security and we also train people that are passionate about becomin full-stack programmers to actualize their goals.

About Us

We certainly believe that technology is one of the keys to Africa's development and as such we are investing time and resources to enable Africans to grow Technologically.

Two years down the line,klemwebhave been able to roll tangible milestones by impacting knowledge to over 100 youngsters both within and outside her domain.Due to the low number of tech companies in Africa and the explosion of digital needs especially post-pandemic we do not have to be worried in the aspect of sales because thousands of companies are hiring tech experts daily.

Our Mission

To train over One Million Youngsters to deploy in-demand skills to bridge the digital gap to meet the growing technological clamour in Africa

To produce over one million ICT Experts in the next 3 years.

To have standard ICT Centers for computer-based-tests(UTME,TOEFL,SAAT,IELTS,etc)

To offer virtual Training for ease of access across the globe.

Our Vision

  To plant Branches all over Africa.

  To make learning easy at affordable Rates.

  To employ first-class tools to raise youngsters that will be at the vanguard of technology across africa and with high flying representation in the world.

  To reposition youngsters mindset towards ethical modalities of modern technology.
